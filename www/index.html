Resources

If you are feeling suicidal, please contact:

American Users:

  • Suicide Prevention Hotline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • HopeLine Suicide Hotline: 1-800-SUICIDE (1-800-784-2433)
  • Call 911

American and Non-American Users:

  • Psychiatric hospital walk-in clinic
  • Hospital emergency room
  • Urgent care center/clinic
  • http://www.suicide.org/international-suicide-hotlines.html
  • http://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
  • http://www.befrienders.org/
  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_suicide_crisis_lines
